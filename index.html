Zebrafish Expression Ontology of Gene Sets (ZEOGS) Input Form

ZEOGS is a tool predicting sites of preferential expression of zebrafish gene sets.

It allows biologists doing experiments on zebrafish to summarize expression of large gene sets using standardised anatomical terms available from the Zebrafish Information Network. In addition, ZEOGS assesses over-representation of each encountered anatomical term relative to occurrence of these anatomical terms among all of known or tested zebrafish genes with expression.

ZEOGS requires the following input for obtaining expression ontology information for your genes:

  • ZFIN ID

    ZFIN Symbol

    • OR
